A. F. Henry

The large amount of data Coursera gathers from 10 mil learners leads to statistically significant insights. Coursera instructor dashboard is one of competitive advantages of the platform; it provides key statistics trend and learner feedback, including courser ratings, learner feedbacks, and demographics to our partners. With these statistics instructors measure the pulse of the course, pinpoint statistic problem spots, and provosts and instructional designers make informed decisions for future courses.

Starting with Course Dashboard design in 2014, I worked on all three Coursera user-facing dashboard products. As the only designer in charge of the projects, I worked on the full stages of process, from needfinding that led to product and metrics desicions, to wireframes, visual design and supporting engineers through implementation.
