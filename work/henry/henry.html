A. F. Henry

A. F. Henry is a high-end bathroom appliances brand conceived in 2015 in San Francisco. The brand cares about beautiful and highly-functional products reflective of the style of modern homes. Callisto bathroom fan is the first product of the brand. The fan is crisp, clean, and quiet as the night sky—the natural inspiration that has guided the development of A.F. Henry Fan Company’s products.

I was commissioned to design the identity, packaging, and brochure for the brand. The identity represents the gentle flow of air and water. The colour reflects the fan's inspiration from the night sky, and along with the classical and minimal packaging sets the tone of a luxury home appliances brand.
