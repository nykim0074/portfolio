Nice to meet you


September 2014, Stanford - photo by Scott MacDonald


My name is Natalie Kim, a UX & visual designer, working in the field over 6 years designing digital and physical experiences.

 

My passion lies in tackling big thorny problems through empathy. I strive to make the system invisible and make the complex simple, by understanding users' goals and mental models.

 

Currently I’m defining partner and learner experience at Content Experience & Teaching team at Coursera. During my time at the world’s leading ed-tech company, I was in charge of the design of several fundamental projects including content authoring tools, analytics dashboards, content rating system, and high-enagament learning. I am the champion of user centered product development process and bringing the culture of empathy to people I work with.
