Nice to meet you


Feb 2012, Brooklyn NYC - photo by Michael O'Neal


My name is Natalie, I’m a UX & visual designer with over 6 years of experience in designing digital and physical experiences.

 

Design has always been my passion and delivering appropriate branding experiences is the base of every project I worked on. I work across different media and platforms, from digital to physical.

 

Currently I’m defining partner experience and bringing up content quality at Content Experience & Teaching team at Coursera During my time at the world’s leading MOOC company, I led the design for Content authoring tools and analytics dashboard for partners, and content rating system. I am the champion of user centered product development process and passionate for design processes as well
